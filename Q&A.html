Q&A

Some recently questions~

What can our club do for new club members?

We will introduce our drivers after providing our members with guide assistance. We will also go over our driving policies and the rules of the club.

Is this club a legal rally cars club?

The answer is "YES," and we were created in the 2006 for enthusiasts of vintage cars.

How many does our club hold races during a year?

The answer varies because we occasionally hold races near mountains, so we must consider the weather conditions specific to those locations. But every year, we hold at least three.

Does our club provide a training for races?

Yes, we also provide training if the member asks for it.
